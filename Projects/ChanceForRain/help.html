Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do the buttons with the logos on the top of the page do?
  •  These are the filters for the DLC. By default, DLC items are shown. Selecting 'Risk of Rain 2' removes them.

  • Is there an easier way to navigate on mobile?
  •  Typing a category of item or game element (i.e: 'common' or 'shrines') into the search bar on the top right will filter everything to only items in those categories, and make scrolling much faster.

  • I found an issue/have further questions/have a problem, how can I contact you?
  •  You can contact me through my email Business@michaelwarmbier.com. Alternatively, you can find me on my Twitter.
